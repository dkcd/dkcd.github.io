Butter Chicken

Delicious North Indian Main Course Dish

Best accompanied with chawal/pulao (rice) or naan/paraatha (breads)

  • Serving size: 4 people
  • Preparation time: 1 hour
  • Difficulty level: Easy

Ingredients:

  1. Chicken breast - 500 gm
  2. Yogurt (preferably Greek) - 1 cup (150 gm)
  3. Tomato puree - 200 gm
  4. Thick cream (30%) - 200 gm
  5. Butter (salted) - 2 tablespoons
  6. Onions - 2 big ones
  7. Garlic - 2 cloves
  8. Olive oil - 5 tablespoons
  9. Chicken masala (powdered spice) - 2 teaspoon
  10. Salt - As required
  11. Black pepper - As required
  12. Red chili powder - As required
  13. Water - As required
